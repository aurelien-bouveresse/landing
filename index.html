The concept

When a user downloads the application, he or she is required to fill in a number of profile details. Here is the list of information to be filled in during registration like Login information: phone, email address, username, password, or General information: Name, first name, gender, age and Specific information like Sport(s) practiced: currently or in the future, Aims of the application: meet partners, find a club or facilities, or Types of facilities required: clubs, available pitches, sports associations... This information allows the user to discover a 100% personalised application thanks to suggestions of ads in line with their needs, such as facilities or clubs that correspond to what they are looking for, and partners close to home.
